# 🍔 Food Ordering Behaviour and Consumer Trends Analysis

An end-to-end data analytics project that explores customer food ordering behaviour, cuisine preferences, meal trends, and revenue patterns using **Tableau**. The project transforms raw food ordering data into interactive dashboards that help businesses understand customer preferences and make data-driven decisions.

---

## 📖 Project Overview

The Food Ordering Behaviour and Consumer Trends Analysis project focuses on analyzing customer ordering patterns across different cities, cuisines, meal types, restaurant categories, and age groups.

The project follows a complete project development lifecycle, beginning with requirement analysis and ending with interactive Tableau dashboards and storytelling.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze customer ordering behaviour.
- Identify the most preferred cuisines.
- Compare cuisine demand across cities.
- Study customer preferences by age group.
- Analyze restaurant type popularity.
- Identify the highest revenue-generating meal types.
- Study customer ratings for different meal categories.
- Analyze delivery time distribution.
- Build an interactive Tableau dashboard for business insights.

# 📊 Dashboard Features

The dashboard provides interactive insights into:

### ✔ Orders by Age Group & Restaurant Type

- Compare ordering behaviour across age groups.
- Compare Budget, Mid-range and Premium restaurants.

---

### ✔ City vs Cuisine Analysis

- Compare cuisine demand across cities.
- Identify popular cuisines in each city using heat maps.

---

### ✔ Company Type & Meal Type Orders

- Analyze food ordering behaviour when customers order

- Alone
- With Friends
- Family
- Partner

---

### ✔ Revenue Contribution by Meal Type

Shows revenue generated by:

- Breakfast
- Lunch
- Dinner
- Snacks

---

### ✔ Cuisine Preference Distribution

Displays customer preference percentage for

- Biryani
- Chinese
- Desserts
- Fast Food
- North Indian
- South Indian

---

### ✔ Meal Type vs Customer Rating

Visualizes customer ratings across meal categories.

---

### ✔ Top Cities by Order Volume

Ranks cities based on total food orders.

---

### ✔ Delivery Time Distribution

Shows order distribution according to delivery time.

---

# 📈 Key Insights

- Millennials and Gen Z place the highest number of food orders.
- Premium restaurants receive significant customer attention.
- Cuisine preference is nearly balanced across all major cuisines.
- Mumbai records the highest order volume.
- Snacks contribute the highest revenue among meal types.
- Delivery times are evenly distributed with slight variations.
- Interactive filters allow users to analyze specific cities, cuisines, and meal types.
